About Us

Table Master:

Made by Rushikesh S. Harkal, Table Master is a great tool for students, kids, and even competitive exam aspirants. It helps you master multiplication tables in a simple and fun way.

Features:

  • Practice Mode: Learn tables step-by-step, at your own pace.
  • Fun Challenges: Timed quizzes make learning exciting and boost your speed.
  • Track Progress: See how much you've improved.
  • Useful for Exams: Perfect for preparing mental math needed in competitive exams.
  • Easy to Use: Simple design that works for everyone.

With Table Master, learning tables is fast, fun, and useful for exams too.
